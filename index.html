Project Brief

We came across this project because of a re-branding issue that no longer reached the target audience. The role for the UX/UI Designers was to put the company’s vision first, but also relay their message through their website.


Compass Connections is a non-profit agency that serves the community’s most vulnerable children and their caregivers. This is their mission statement, and a principle they live by. We chose to redesign this website due to the positive impact the agency has on the community.
